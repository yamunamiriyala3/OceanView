RushiKonda Beach

Rushikonda Beach, located near Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh, is a stunning destination known for its golden sands and clear blue waters. Nestled amidst lush green hills, it's a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, offering activities like swimming, windsurfing, and water sports.

Anjuna Beach

Anjuna Beach, located in North Goa, is famous for its vibrant atmosphere, golden sands, and palm-fringed coastline. Known for its lively flea markets, beach parties, and water sports, it is a hotspot for tourists seeking a mix of relaxation and entertainment.

Ramakrishna Beach

RK Beach, or Ramakrishna Beach, located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, is a vibrant coastal destination along the Bay of Bengal. Known for its scenic beauty, the beach offers a mix of relaxation and recreation, with attractions like the INS Kursura Submarine Museum and the Kali Temple nearby.

Vishakapatnam
20°C Sunny

Butterfly Beach

Butterfly Beach, located in South Goa, is a secluded paradise known for its crescent-shaped shoreline, pristine golden sand, and crystal-clear waters. Accessible primarily by boat or trek, it offers tranquil beauty, stunning sunsets, and occasional sightings of dolphins and butterflies.

South Goa
27°C Sunny

Calangute Beach

Calangute Beach, located in North Goa, is one of the most popular and lively beaches in India. Known as the "Queen of Beaches," it offers a bustling atmosphere with golden sands, water sports, beach shacks, and vibrant nightlife.

North Goa

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach, located in Kerala, is a world-renowned coastal destination famous for its crescent-shaped shoreline, golden sands, and calm, azure waters. Comprised of three main beaches—Lighthouse, Hawah, and Samudra—it offers stunning views, water sports, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic treatments.

Kerala
22°C Sunny
